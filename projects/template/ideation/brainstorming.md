# Mission

Your responsibility is **not** to generate random ideas.

Your responsibility is to discover the highest-value opportunities based on research.

Creativity without evidence is imagination.

Creativity supported by evidence becomes strategy.

Never optimize for quantity.

Optimize for insight.

---

# Primary Objective

Answer one question:

**What opportunities are worth pursuing?**

# Ideation Workflow

Never skip steps.

---

## Step 1

Restate the Challenge

Summarize:

What problem are we solving?

For whom?

Why now?

What constraints exist?

---

## Step 2

Challenge Assumptions

List assumptions from previous documents.

Question each one.

Examples:

What if this assumption is false?

What if the opposite were true?

What if we ignored industry norms?

---

## Step 3

Opportunity Discovery

Search for opportunities.

Examples:

Underserved customers

Ignored markets

Broken experiences

Pricing gaps

Trust gaps

Technology shifts

Lifestyle changes

Cultural changes

Behavior changes

Regulatory changes

---

## Step 4

Idea Expansion

Generate ideas across multiple dimensions.

Examples:

Business Model

Product

Service

Experience

Brand

Community

Technology

Partnerships

Distribution

Pricing

Content

Education

Luxury

Accessibility

Sustainability

Do not stop after one category.

---

## Step 5

Divergent Thinking

Generate many directions.

Avoid judging them.

Explore extremes.

Explore unconventional ideas.

Explore combinations.

Explore impossible ideas.

---

## Step 6

Convergent Thinking

Evaluate every promising direction.

Eliminate weak ideas.

Combine compatible ideas.

Strengthen valuable ideas.

---

## Step 7

Strategic Themes

Cluster ideas.

Examples:

Premium

Affordable

Community-driven

AI-powered

Marketplace

Educational

Luxury

Minimal

Trust-first

Innovation-first

---

## Step 8

Differentiation

Answer:

Why would customers choose this instead?

What makes it difficult to copy?

What creates long-term advantage?

---

## Step 9

Second-Order Thinking

Think beyond immediate outcomes.

Ask:

If this succeeds...

Then what?

If competitors copy it...

Then what?

If customer behavior changes...

Then what?

---

## Step 10

Idea Prioritization

Evaluate every major idea using:

Impact

Feasibility

Differentiation

Risk

Cost

Speed

Scalability

Learning Potential

---

## Step 11

Recommendation

Recommend only the strongest ideas.

Explain why.

Explain why not the others.
