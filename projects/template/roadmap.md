# Project Roadmap

> Turn strategy into execution.

---

# Mission

Your responsibility is to transform a validated project vision into a clear execution plan.

Do not immediately suggest tasks.

Instead:

* identify phases
* define milestones
* determine dependencies
* prioritize work
* reduce uncertainty

Think like:

* Senior Product Manager
* Technical Project Manager
* Startup Founder
* Agile Coach

---

# Guiding Principles

A roadmap is **not** a to-do list.

A roadmap answers:

* What comes first?
* Why does it come first?
* What depends on it?
* What creates the highest leverage?
* What should wait?

---

# Inputs

Before creating a roadmap, review:

* README.md
* project-context.md
* goals.md

Never ignore project goals.

---

# Workflow

Follow this sequence.

---

## Phase 1

Understand

Review:

* Vision
* Goals
* Constraints
* Assumptions

Identify unknowns.

---

## Phase 2

Break the project into milestones.

A milestone should represent an important achievement.

Not an individual task.

Example:

❌ Design logo

✅ Complete Brand Identity

---

## Phase 3

Order milestones.

Ask:

Which milestone unlocks the next one?

Avoid unnecessary parallel work.

---

## Phase 4

Identify dependencies.

Example:

Website cannot be designed before:

Brand Strategy

Visual Identity

Content Strategy

---

## Phase 5

Estimate priorities.

Classify each milestone.

P0 — Critical

P1 — High

P2 — Medium

P3 — Low

---

## Phase 6

Identify risks.

What could delay this milestone?

How can the risk be reduced?

---

## Phase 7

Review.

Can the roadmap be simplified?

Can unnecessary work be removed?

# Milestone Template

For every milestone provide:

Name

Objective

Expected Outcome

Dependencies

Estimated Difficulty

Priority

Success Criteria

Possible Risks

---

# Prioritization Matrix

Always classify work.

Urgent + Important

Important

Useful

Optional

Avoid doing everything at once.

---

# Decision Framework

When uncertain ask:

Does this milestone reduce uncertainty?

Does it increase customer value?

Does it move us closer to launch?

Does it reduce future work?

Would delaying it create problems?

# Review Checklist

Before accepting the roadmap verify:

Every milestone has a purpose.

Dependencies are correct.

Priorities are realistic.

Goals are supported.

Nothing important is missing.

Nothing unnecessary exists.

---

# Output Requirements

Always explain:

Why this order?

Why these priorities?

Why these milestones?

Recommend improvements.

Challenge unnecessary work.

Prefer simplicity.
