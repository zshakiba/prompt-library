# Project Template

A reusable AI-powered project framework for planning, researching, validating, branding, and executing ideas.

---

# Overview

This template provides a structured workflow for developing any idea into a well-defined project using AI.

Instead of relying on one long prompt, the project is divided into specialized stages. Each stage focuses on solving one problem well and produces artifacts that become inputs for the next stage.

The framework is inspired by modern AI collaboration workflows, where AI acts as a strategist, researcher, critic, and execution partner—not just a text generator.

---

# Philosophy

Good projects are rarely built by finding the right answer immediately.

They are built through cycles of:

* Exploration
* Research
* Evaluation
* Refinement
* Decision Making

This template encourages structured thinking instead of random prompting.

# File Responsibilities

## project-context.md

Defines the project.

Contains:

* Vision
* Background
* Problem
* Opportunity
* Current assumptions
* Available information

---

## goals.md

Defines success.

Examples:

* Business goals
* Product goals
* User goals
* Technical goals
* Long-term vision

---

## constraints.md

Lists project limitations.

Examples:

* Budget
* Time
* Technology
* Legal restrictions
* Team size
* Geographic limitations

---

## roadmap.md

Transforms goals into milestones.

Contains:

* Phases
* Priorities
* Deliverables
* Timeline
* Dependencies

---

## research/

Collect evidence before making decisions.

Includes:

* Market Research
* Competitor Research
* Audience Research

---

## ideation/

Expand possibilities before choosing one.

Includes:

* Brainstorming
* Naming
* Validation

---

## branding/

Build a consistent identity.

Includes:

* Brand Strategy
* Visual Identity

---

## marketing/

Create the launch strategy.

Includes:

* Instagram Strategy
* Website Strategy

---

## review/

Challenge every important assumption.

Look for:

* Weak ideas
* Missing information
* Risks
* Blind spots

---

## final.md

The final project summary.

This document combines all validated decisions into one place.

# Recommended Workflow

Always work in this order:

1. Define Context
2. Define Goals
3. Research
4. Brainstorm
5. Validate
6. Build Brand
7. Create Marketing Strategy
8. Critique Everything
9. Produce Final Version

Avoid skipping steps.

Good decisions require good context.

---

# Working with AI

Treat AI as a collaborative team instead of a chatbot.

Different stages require different roles.

Examples:

* Research Analyst
* Product Manager
* Brand Strategist
* UX Designer
* Marketing Expert
* Startup Advisor
* Critical Reviewer

Ask AI to switch roles when entering a new stage.

---

# Guiding Principles

* Think before generating.
* Explore before deciding.
* Validate before committing.
* Prefer evidence over assumptions.
* Document every important decision.
* Make decisions reversible whenever possible.
* Keep artifacts reusable.

---
