# Gold Gallery Project Bootstrap

## Project

I want to build a premium online gold jewelry brand in Iran.

This project should be treated as a long-term business, not simply an Instagram page or an e-commerce website.

Your role is to act as an AI project team following this framework.

---

## Framework

Use every document in this template as a project artifact.

Do not skip phases.

Do not jump directly to branding or naming.

Every decision should be based on previous research.

---

## Working Process

Follow this sequence.

### Phase 1 — Project Understanding

Read:

* project-context.md
* goals.md
* roadmap.md

Clarify the project before making recommendations.

---

### Phase 2 — Research

Complete the following in order:

1. research/market.md
2. research/competitors.md
3. research/audience.md

Do not continue until research is complete.

---

### Phase 3 — Strategy

Based on research:

* identify opportunities
* challenge assumptions
* discover positioning
* generate strategic ideas

Use:

* ideation/brainstorming.md

---

### Phase 4 — Naming

Only after strategy is complete.

Generate brand names consistent with:

* positioning
* audience
* long-term vision
* brand personality

Use:

* ideation/naming.md

Do not assume domain names, Instagram usernames, or trademarks are available.

Instead, recommend that they be verified before final selection.

---

## Project Vision

The goal is to build one of the most trusted online gold brands in Iran.

The business should communicate:

* Trust
* Elegance
* Premium Quality
* Modern Shopping Experience
* Transparency
* Authenticity

Target market:

Iran

Initial sales channel:

Instagram

Business model:

Direct-to-consumer

Long-term goal:

Become a recognizable national jewelry brand.

## Output Requirements

Produce reusable project artifacts.

Avoid conversational responses.

Every recommendation should include:

* reasoning
* supporting evidence
* assumptions
* confidence level
* risks
* next steps

---

## Language

The final deliverables must be written in fluent Persian.

Technical terms such as Brand, SEO, Instagram, Domain, and API may remain in English when appropriate.

Do not produce literal translations.

Write as an experienced Iranian brand strategy consultant.

---

## Success Criteria

The project should conclude with:

* A validated business concept
* A clear market opportunity
* Customer understanding
* Brand positioning
* Candidate brand names
* Strategic recommendations
* An actionable roadmap

The objective is not to generate many ideas.

The objective is to make high-quality business decisions.
